Supported Formats

ESPN Box Score Format

Copy the entire box score including team names, the "PLAYER MIN FGM..." header, all player rows, and the TOTALS line. Include the Inactive Players section if available.

What Gets Captured

  • Player stats: PTS, REB, AST, MIN, FG%, 3P%, FT%, STL, BLK, TO, +/-
  • Player positions (G, F, C)
  • Team totals
  • DNP/DND players with reasons
  • Inactive players list

🏀 Import Matchups

Paste the schedule/matchups data including spreads and over/under lines. The system will parse teams, times, and betting lines.

Matchup Format

Copy the schedule directly from ESPN or similar. Include:

  • Date header (e.g., "Saturday, January 3, 2026")
  • Away team @ Home team
  • Game time
  • Line: TEAM -X.X (spread)
  • O/U: XXX.X (total)

🏥 Import Injury Report

Paste the official NBA injury report. The system will parse player statuses and factor injuries into projections.

Injury Report Impact

Injuries affect projections in several ways:

  • OUT players: Usage redistributed to remaining players
  • Questionable: Highlighted in analysis as risk factors
  • Star player out: Major boost to teammates' projections
  • Elite defender out: Boosts opponent scoring projections
